Towels

Towels2/27/16

Sometimes by welcoming someone new into my life, I can learn something about myself.

Autumn's End

Finally, sleep.

Summer trees sip soup

And pull their yellow comforters

Up to their chins.

Your Mystery

Lover, open your mouth so I can check your teeth

And peer into your pupils and ear canals with a tiny light

Let me dissect the stubborn contents of your skull

And push my lonely body against yours

Embrace me

Please

So I can start to understand.
